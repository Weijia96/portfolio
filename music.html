Highlights

Dream

Dream is one of the three works I would like the Admissions Committee to review. My inspiration for this audio comes from one of my crazy dreams. The dream is not real, so this project allows me to try different audio elements that I don’t usually use. In the dream, I had a fantastic journey between reality and my imagination. I tried to use audio to portray the abstract images and crazy adventure.  

Intense

Intense is also one of the three works I would like the Admissions Committee to review.This piece describes an extreme emotion. The idea of this piece is from the movie Misery. In this movie, Annie was the “number one” fan of Paul who was a famous writer. Annie was crazy for Paul. She imprisoned Paul. Her love for Paul was sometimes gentle, sometimes intense, and sometimes horrific. I want to use this piece of music to express the spooky love that Annie had to Paul. 

Emotion Tree

Emotion tree is the last one of the three works I would like the Admissions Committee to review. After my research on human emotion. I learned that music is powerful to get emotional responses. I used this idea in the concept of my web design. The web page has six doors and each door represents an emotion. Users can experience the emotional expressions of the brain on each door. The purpose of this project was to provide users a better understanding of emotions. (This website need a little bit time to load the audio).
